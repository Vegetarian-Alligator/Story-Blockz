Don't forget, before you start! There are two ways to save your game with important differences! To save your game to work on it later you have to hit "save." To play your game, hit "export." If you only play your game and forget to save it, you'll never be able to edit it again! You'll have to start over!
Know that when typing things for the game, you can't type '[' unless it is part of one of the commands!
